## Multi-party collaborative multi-objective optimization

> Definition: Multiple parties collaborate to complete the overall multi-objective optimization task. At the same time, each party also has its own multi-objective optimization task. It is necessary to complete the overall task on the basis of completing their own tasks. This is called multi-party collaborative multi-objective optimization.
